# HY3N Rider App - Complete Feature List

## 🚗 Ride Booking & Management

### Core Booking Features
- **Instant Ride Booking** - Book a ride in under 30 seconds
- **Scheduled Rides** - Reserve rides up to 30 days in advance
- **Multiple Ride Categories**:
  - HY3N Standard (affordable, 4 seats)
  - HY3N Comfort (premium comfort, 4 seats)
  - Kantanka HY3N (Ghanaian-made, 4 seats)
  - HY3N Executive (luxury, 4 seats)
  - HY3N Express Delivery (package delivery)
- **Real-Time Fare Estimation** - See exact price before booking
- **Dynamic Surge Pricing** - Transparent multipliers during high demand
- **Distance & Duration Display** - Know your trip details upfront
- **Saved Locations** - Quick access to Home, Work, and custom locations
- **Recent Destinations** - One-tap rebooking from history
- **Popular Places** - Curated list of common destinations in Ghana

### Ride Experience
- **Live Driver Tracking** - Watch your driver approach on the map
- **Accurate ETAs** - Powered by Google Maps routing
- **Driver Information** - Name, photo, vehicle details, license plate
- **Real-Time Trip Updates** - Status notifications at every stage
- **In-App Messaging** - Contact driver directly
- **Trip Sharing** - Send ride details to friends/family for safety
- **SOS Emergency Button** - Instant alert to emergency contacts and admins
- **Route Optimization** - Best routes calculated automatically
